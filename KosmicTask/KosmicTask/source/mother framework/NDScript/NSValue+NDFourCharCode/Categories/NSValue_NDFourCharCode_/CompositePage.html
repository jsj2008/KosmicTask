NSValue(NDFourCharCode)

Methods for creating instances of NSValue to represent any sub-types of FourCharCode.

Extends Class: NSValue
Declared In: NSValue+NDFourCharCode.h

Discussion

FourCharCode are simply unsigned long int but are usually represented as 4 chars for example 'pnam'.
