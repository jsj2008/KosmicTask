You might need to perform the following operations at installation time in order for F-Script Anywhere to work on your system. 

In Terminal, enter: cd /path/to/F-Script\ Anywhere.app/Contents/MacOS", replacing "/path/to/F-Script\ Anywhere.app" by the actual path to F-Script Anywhere.app on your computer.

Enter : sudo chmod g+s F-Script\ Anywhere

Enter : sudo chgrp procmod F-Script\ Anywhere

You can now launch F-Script Anywhere.
